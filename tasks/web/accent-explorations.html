Recommendation Ship C · accent on CTAs + selection · success-green for completion

Two semantic roles for color in the workout chrome — accent = "what's active / where I am", success = "what's done". This protects user-picked accents (pink, orange) from looking aggressive while staying useful. Mode A is what most fitness apps do and feels gym-bro by default; B reads too sterile.
